Eternal Rome: Where History Breathes

Things to See and Do

Ancient Rome

Colosseum & Roman Forum - Best visited early morning (8:30 AM) or late afternoon. Book "Full Experience" ticket for access to Arena Floor and Underground. Combine with Palatine Hill (included in same ticket). Consider guided tour for historical context - the ruins come alive with stories. Pro tip: Enter through Palatine Hill entrance for shorter queues.

Pantheon - Visit during midday to see the sun beam through the oculus. Free entry, but book time slot online. Still functions as a church - be respectful. Best preserved ancient Roman building. Nearby: Best coffee at Sant'Eustachio Il Caff√®.

Vatican Highlights

Vatican Museums & Sistine Chapel - Book "early entrance" tickets (worth the extra cost). Must-sees: Raphael Rooms, Map Gallery, Sistine Chapel. Plan 3-4 hours minimum. Pro tip: Take the secret door from Sistine Chapel to St. Peter's (if available, only with guide).

St. Peter's Basilica - Free entry, but expect long security lines. Climb the dome early morning for best views and shorter queues. Dress code strictly enforced - no bare shoulders or knees. Wednesday mornings: Papal audience (free but requires tickets).
