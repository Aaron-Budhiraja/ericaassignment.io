About

Hello, I'm Erica

As a passionate travel blogger and photographer, I've spent the last decade exploring the hidden corners of our beautiful world. Born in London but raised with a nomadic spirit, I've always been drawn to the stories that lie beneath the surface of every destination I visit.

My journey began in 2013 when I left my corporate job in finance to pursue my true passion - storytelling through travel. What started as a personal blog to keep my family updated has grown into a community of fellow adventurers who share my love for authentic travel experiences.

I believe that travel is more than just visiting new places; it's about immersing yourself in different cultures, connecting with local communities, and challenging your own perspectives. Whether I'm sampling street food in Hong Kong, exploring ancient temples in Moscow, or wandering through Madrid's art galleries, I strive to capture the essence of each destination through my lens and words.

When I'm not on the road, you can find me in my home base in London, where I host travel photography workshops and writing seminars for aspiring bloggers. I'm also working on my first travel photography book, which will showcase the intimate moments and hidden gems I've discovered throughout my journeys.

Through this blog, I hope to inspire others to step out of their comfort zones and experience the transformative power of travel. Every destination has a story to tell, and I'm here to help you discover it.
